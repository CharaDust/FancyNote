#### Basic formula:
$$
\begin{align*}
\mathrm{in}{~x=0~}\\
{~f(x)~}=&&{~f(0)~}&&
{~+\frac{~f'(0)~}{1!}x~}&&
{~+\frac{~f''(0)~}{2!}x^2~}&&
{~+\frac{~f'''(0)~}{3!}x^3~}&&
{~+\underline{o}(x^3)~}
\end{align*}
$$
