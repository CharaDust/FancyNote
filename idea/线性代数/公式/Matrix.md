#### SUM & DIFF of Powers:
$$
\begin{align}
x^n - y^n = (x - y)\left( x^{n-1} + x^{n-2}y + x^{n-3}y^2 + \cdots + xy^{n-2} + y^{n-1} \right) \\
x^n + y^n = (x + y)\left( x^{n-1} - x^{n-2}y + x^{n-3}y^2 - \cdots - xy^{n-2} + y^{n-1} \right)&& \quad (n \text{ is odd})
\end{align}
